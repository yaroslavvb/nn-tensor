The interactive paper decks (start or finish with these)

Three slide-deck walkthroughs built from the primary papers, with live widgets and every number extracted from the source (← → to navigate):

deckGenerative AI for Crystal Structures — an interactive tour

17 slides · from De Breuck, Wang, Rignanese, Botti & Marques, arXiv:2509.02723 (review)

The field map: CSP-vs-generative paradigm, representations & invariances, the architecture zoo, an explorable catalog of all 61 models from the paper's taxonomy, datasets, metrics, and headline generated-materials results.

deckGNoME: Scaling Deep Learning for Materials Discovery

15 slides · from Merchant et al., Nature 623, 80–85 (2023)

The scaled-screening flagship: 2.2M new stable structures, a draggable convex-hull explainer, the SAPS + compositional pipelines, the active-learning flywheel animated with the paper's real hit-rate gains, scaling laws, and the zero-shot pretrained potential.

deckLLaMat: Foundational LLMs for Materials Research

16 slides · from Mishra et al., arXiv:2412.09560

The LLM route: CPT → IFT → PEFT on 31.5B tokens, materials-NLP and table-extraction benchmarks vs GPT-4o/Claude/Gemini, LLaMat-CIF crystal generation, and the adaptation-rigidity finding.

Also on this site, for the mathematical backbone of low-rank structure: the tensor-decompositions tutorial, the CP-vs-TT depth-separation deep dive, the worst-case approximation report, the Gaussian-noise page, and the angular-error page.

Step 3

Tutorial-grade papers, in reading order

7paperHas generative artificial intelligence solved inverse materials design?

Park, Li & Walsh · Matter (Cell Press) 2024 · open access · ✓ verified

Compact expert-level review of the whole generative-crystal arc — GANs/VAEs through autoregressive and diffusion — focused on representations and evaluation.

8paperCDVAE: Crystal Diffusion Variational Autoencoder

Xie, Fu, Ganea, Barzilay, Jaakkola · ICLR 2022 · ✓ verified

The pedagogically cleanest origin paper: why periodicity + invariances make crystal generation hard and how a VAE+diffusion hybrid handles it. Everything since defines itself against it.

9paperDiffCSP: Crystal Structure Prediction by Joint Equivariant Diffusion

Jiao et al. · NeurIPS 2023 · ✓ verified

Full periodic-E(3) equivariant diffusion over lattice + fractional coordinates — the modern template.

10paperA Hitchhiker's Guide to Geometric GNNs for 3D Atomic Systems

Duval, Mathis, Joshi, …, Bengio, Bronstein · 2023–24 · ✓ verified

Explicitly pedagogical guide to the invariant/equivariant architectures every crystal generative model builds on. Complement: Reiser et al., GNNs for materials science and chemistry ✓.
